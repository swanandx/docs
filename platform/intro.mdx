## Welcome to Bytebeam!

If you are new to Bytebeam, follow the below sequence to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account on Bytebeam Cloud">
  If you are new to [Bytebeam](https://bytebeam.io), the first step would be to [Getting started on Bytebeam Cloud](docId\:EskOpEZwliCX2CvVeL61e)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understanding Concepts">
  Once you have your account handy go through the [Concepts](docId:1cBb45ora-4NKSwkqJfOf) section to understand how Bytebeam works.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate Bytebeam with a hardware module">
  Module Integration guides section has guides for various hardware modules.

  If you do not find the hardware you are using and need support with integrations, drop us a note at [support@bytebeam.io]().
  </Step>
</Steps>

Bytebeam is a no-code backend solution that you can use to:

- Collect, Store, and Visualize device telemetry.
- Remotely send commands and receive responses from your devices
- Remotely diagnose issues in your device
- Remotely update your devices.
